Strawberry Rhubarb Crumble

8-12 servings; adapted from 101 Cookbooks

  • Butter for greasing skillet/pan (about 1 tablespoon)
  • 3/4 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon fine grain sea salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 lb hulled medium strawberries, cut into quarters
  • 12 ounces trimmed rhubarb, sliced into 3/4-inch pieces

Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F.

Combine the flour, oats, sugar, salt, and pepper together in a bowl. Stir in the butter and form the mixture into small chunks. Spread these out on a sheet of wax paper and chill for 20 minutes in the freezer.

Mix the strawberries and rhubarb together. Add the cornstarch and sugar and toss until the fruits are evenly coated. Add this filling to a buttered baking dish.

Crumble the chilled chunks over the filling. Try to have a mix of sizes, but make sure the entire top is covered.

Bake until the topping is deeply golden. This should take 35-40 minutes. Let cool slightly, but you'll want to serve this warm.
